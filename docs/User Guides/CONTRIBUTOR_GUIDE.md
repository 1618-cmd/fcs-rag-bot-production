## What is a Contributor

Contributors complete Input Tasks, enter data, and submit information for review in Vena. They work with workflows, Input Tasks, and Line Item Details on a daily basis.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific About Your Task:** Instead of "How do I use Vena?", try "How do I complete an Input Task in Vena?"

2. **Mention Your Platform:** Specify whether you are using Vena Desktop or Vena 365 when relevant

3. **Include Context:** If asking about a specific feature, describe what you are trying to accomplish

4. **Ask Follow-up Questions:** If an answer is not clear, ask the bot to explain further or provide an example

5. **Check Sources:** Review the source citations provided by the bot to learn more about the topic

## When the Bot Cannot Help

If the bot cannot answer your question:

1. Your question may be about client-specific workflows or configurations not in the knowledge base
2. The issue may require access to your specific Vena instance to diagnose
3. The problem may be related to permissions or access rights

**What to do:**
- Ask your manager or a Modeler about client-specific configurations
- Create a Jira ticket if you encounter an error or system issue
- Check with your team if the workflow or task configuration is standard
