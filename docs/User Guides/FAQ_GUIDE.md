## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific:** Instead of "How do I use Vena?", try "How do I complete an Input Task in Vena?"

2. **Use Keywords:** Include specific Vena terms like "Line Item Details", "Scope", "VenaQL", or "Workflow" in your questions

3. **Ask Follow-ups:** If an answer is not clear, ask the bot to explain further or provide an example

4. **Check Sources:** Review the source citations to learn more about the topic

5. **Rephrase:** If you do not get a good answer, try asking the question in a different way

## When the Bot Cannot Help

If the bot cannot answer your question:

1. The information may not be in the knowledge base yet
2. Your question may be too specific or client-specific
3. The question may require configuration details not covered in documentation

**What to do:**
- Create a Jira ticket for support
- Ask a colleague who may have encountered the same issue
- Check the official Vena documentation or support resources
