Release overview
All of the other exciting features included with this release
Vena 365 - Template Performance Reporting Tool: Unlock valuable insights into refresh
times and optimize your template design for peak performance with our latest
enhancements. This feature not only informs customers about refresh times but also
empowers them to make proactive and positive changes to their templates, ensuring optimal
functionality and user experience. Visit this article for more information on the Template
Performance Reporting Tool.
Vena Insights - Drill Through Transactions: Empower users with enhanced analytical
capabilities by introducing the "Drill Through" feature. Now, with a simple click on any
enabled Vena Insights report, users can seamlessly access a CSV export of transactional data.
This functionality facilitates a smooth transition between macro-level concepts and micro-
level insights, enabling you to swiftly identify areas that require attention. Streamline your
analytical workflow and gain deeper insights effortlessly. Visit this article for more
information on Drill Through Transactions in Vena Insights.
Vena Knowledge Base Redesign: Our redesigned Knowledge Base boasts a clean, modern
interface that enhances navigation and readability. Browse our most popular articles, access
our Vena Community to interact with other users and use our search function to find content
that is relevant to you. The redesigned knowledge base will launch by March 1, 2024.
