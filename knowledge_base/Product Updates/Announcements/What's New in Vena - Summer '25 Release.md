On-Demand MQL Agent: Natural Language to Expression in Seconds
Simply describe what data you want to export in natural language, and the pluggable MQL Agent
instantly returns a syntax-ready expression, complete with hierarchy references and ready to
copy and paste into any Vena feature. For Closed Beta, the MQL Agent will be available in the ETL
Export page.
Please reach out to lfaria-fichelli@venacorp.com to participate in the beta.
Learn more about using MQL Agent to build MQL expressions.
Planning Agent: AI-Powered Budgeting and Forecasting in Excel
Planning Agent brings the power of Vena Copilot directly into Excel, helping finance teams plan
smarter and faster. With simple, user-directed prompts you can create budgets and forecasts
based on historical data and drivers—no complex formulas or mappings required. Whether
you're spreading costs evenly, applying growth rates or adjusting for inflation, Planning Agent
enhances your workflow with intelligent, AI-driven recommendations – all within Excel.
Please reach out to nlee@venacorp.com to participate in the beta.
