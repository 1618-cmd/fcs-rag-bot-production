Overview
Vena recently identified a compatibility issue affecting the Vena 365 Excel Add-in. This issue is
related to specific versions of Excel and may impact your experience with Vena templates. This
issue may impact Vena 365 customers using Excel version 2504 (released on May 06, 2025) or
above.
Learn more about how to find your Microsoft Excel version.
Instructions for IT on how to avoid or resolve a service disruption.
Q & A
We’re pleased to share that the Excel compatibility issue impacting Vena 365 users
has now beenfully resolved across all tenants.
The platform is once again fully compatible with the affected Excel versions, and the
previously recommended workarounds, such as switching update channels or
manually rolling back Excel versions, are no longer necessary.
We appreciate your patience and understanding as we worked to resolve the issue.

User Audits & Vena Support UserWhat is the change or issue?
Vena users report a general system disruption in Vena 365 Excel, version 2504 and above.
Specifically, users report that templates are crashing when opening a report in Vena 365.
Why is this happening?
This is due to an update to how Excel handles writing null values in cells during a template
refresh. In Excel, a null value is a cell with no content: no zero, text, or formulas.
Affected Versions:
Any build of Excel version 2504 and above. Excel version 2504 was released on May 06, 2025.
Update Channels impacted include Current Channel and Current Channel (Preview), Learn
more here about Update Channels.
Safe Versions:
Monthly Enterprise Channel (currently on version 2502) is not affected.
How to Check Your Excel Version:
You can learn more about how to find your Microsoft Excel version in the Vena knowledge
base.
