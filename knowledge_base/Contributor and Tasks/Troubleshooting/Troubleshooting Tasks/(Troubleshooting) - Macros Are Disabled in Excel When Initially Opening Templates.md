Suggested solution
1. Add vena.io as a trusted site in Internet Explorer by following the steps in the following
article: Add Vena to trusted sites.
2. Launch Excel, navigate to Options>Trust Center>Trust Center Settings...>Macro Settings.
3. Under Macro Settings, select the option for 'Disable all macros with notification' and under
