Suggested solution
Before you begin, you must have Manager access to follow the steps in this solution. If you do
not have Manager access, please contact a Vena Admin or Manager in your organization to
solve this issue.
1. Open the template.
2. Select Analyze Template.
3. Fix any mapping errors in the template.
4. Ask the Contributor to open the template and try again.
