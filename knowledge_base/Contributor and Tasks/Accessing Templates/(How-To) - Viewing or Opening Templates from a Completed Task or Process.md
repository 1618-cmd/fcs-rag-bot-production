Product UpdatesWhy use this feature?
It can be helpful to reference reports or templates from a task or process that are already
completed. This allows you to gather important information that you need for your records.
Before you begin
To follow the instructions in this article, you will need at least Contributor access.
How to
View or Open Templates from a Completed Task or Process
1. Navigate to the Tasks tab.
2. By default, the view will be set to In Play. Select Process Filter: In Play to see the additional
options.
