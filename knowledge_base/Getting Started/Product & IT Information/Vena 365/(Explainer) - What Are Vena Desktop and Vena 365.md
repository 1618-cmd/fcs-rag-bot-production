How do Vena and Microsoft work together?
Vena is a comprehensive planning platform natively integrated with Microsoft 365, including
Excel and Power BI. Vena’s partnership and integration with Microsoft provide a simple and
familiar way to efficiently plan and budget your finances.  Vena offers and supports two Excel
Add-ins:
Vena Desktop
Vena 365
These Add-ins connect your Excel workbooks to Vena’s cloud-based data models, enabling you
to store, retrieve and manage financial data effortlessly.

Product UpdatesVena Desktop and Vena 365 are Excel Add-ins designed to streamline financial planning and
budgeting. Both Add-ins allow users to create Excel workbooks tailored to your unique financial
needs, with seamless connections to Vena’s cloud-based data models.
Vena Desktop: Works with Windows.
Vena 365: Built for the latest Microsoft 365 suite, supporting both Windows and Mac. While
Vena 365 may work with older versions of Excel, we may be unable to resolve issues that
arise from earlier versions.
With Vena 365, users benefit from Microsoft’s ongoing innovations in Microsoft 365, ensuring
access to the latest features and improvements.
