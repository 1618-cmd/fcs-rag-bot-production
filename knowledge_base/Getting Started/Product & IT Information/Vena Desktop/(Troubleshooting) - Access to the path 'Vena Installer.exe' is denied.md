Issue summary
When installing Vena Desktop, you may encounter the following error:
System.UnauthorizedAccessException: Access to the path 'Vena Installer.exe' is denied.
at System. I0. Directory.DeleteHelper(String fullPath, String userPath, Boolean recursive, Boolean throwonTopLevelDiredFound.
Suggested solution
1. Contact your internal IT team and ensure you have all the necessary permissions.
2. Once your permissions have been updated, close all Microsoft Office applications (Outlook, Teams, Excel, Word and PowerPoint) that are running on the
system.
3. Re-install Vena Desktop.
4. If your permissions cannot be updated for any reason, you can also ask your IT admin to install Vena Desktop on your behalf via the MSI version. Visit the How-
To article on Installing and Updating the MSI Version of Vena Desktop for more information.
