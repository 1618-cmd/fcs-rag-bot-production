Microsoft Account to Vena
Ensure that the correct Microsoft account is linked to Vena for a smooth experience.
Why use this feature?
If your Microsoft account is not linked to Vena, or an incorrect Microsoft account is linked to
Vena, you may experience issues opening and working with your Vena templates in Excel Online.
These instructions ensure you can unlink and link the appropriate Microsoft account with Vena.
