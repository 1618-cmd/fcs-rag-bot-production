Submit product feature requests, vote on your favorite topics, and monitor the status

Why use this feature?
At Vena, we value product feature requests and view them as an essential part of our product
growth. Customer feedback is crucial in understanding your priorities and shaping our product's
direction and strategy. By sharing your requests and suggestions, you contribute to enhancing
our product for the benefit of all users and pinpointing areas of highest significance.

Product UpdatesWith Vena’s product feature request portal, you can:
Submit a feature request by filling out a short form to explain what you are trying to achieve.
Describe any current workarounds.
Browse, comment, and vote on requests submitted by other users.
Stay updated on the latest features via the “Releases” section.
You can review the status of your request and any requests you have voted on at any time. If
you have additional information or questions about your request, feel free to leave a comment,
and a member of the Vena product team will be sure to review and respond.
Before you begin
To follow this article's instructions, log in to a Vena tenant. All Vena users can submit product
feature requests.

Our product team reviews product feature requests monthly and will move them into one of the
following statuses:
Awaiting Feedback (most common): In this stage, your request is published, and you can
gather votes from other customers to better understand its priority and interest.
Planned: This request aligns with work already planned for an upcoming release.
Declined: This status indicates one of the following:
A duplicate request has already been submitted. In this case, we will link the requests and
direct you to a single version of the truth.
This request does not align with Vena’s product vision.
Our product has already fulfilled this request (details will be provided).
This request is a bug or a question and has already been routed to our support team.
