As with any other AI chatbot, the more details you give Vena Help, the better. By including details
such as specific error codes, modules, template types and goals you’re trying to achieve, Vena
Help can better assist you.

Vena Academy is our online learning management system. With Vena Academy, you can brush
up on your skills, explore new ways to use Vena and enroll in micro-learns about different

The Knowledge Base contains hundreds of articles to help you learn more about Vena. Use the
Knowledge Base to gain access to hundreds of how-to, explainer or troubleshooting articles.
When the Knowledge Base opens in a new tab, Vena Help automatically authenticates you,
giving you access to all of the material. If you are not automatically authenticated, selecting the
Sign In button authenticates you if you are logged into Vena.
Connect with Vena Support
If you need additional help, enter “Connect withan agent” in the chat. You will be entered into a
queue until the next available agent is ready. While you are waiting to be connected to an agent,
do not log out, open a new tab or restart the chat.

Notes & limitations
The chat will automatically end if you log out or if your Vena session becomes inactive.
The following actions will end the chatbot session: logging in and out of Vena, opening a new
tab or if your vena.io session becomes inactive.
Vena Help chat history will remain for the duration of your session.
Your chat does not carry over if you relaunch the chatbot in the Knowledge Base.31/12/2025, 12:14 How-To: Using Vena Help – Vena Solutions
