How to
Change supported language on the Vena platform
1. Login to Vena.
2. Select the language menu next to the Help icon in the header. Choose your language from
English (US), European French (FR-FR), French Canadian (FR-CA), German (DE) or Spanish (ES).31/12/2025, 12:23 How-To: Enable Multi-Language Support – Vena Solutions
