Access Vena Academy
Once you have filled out the Vena Academy request form, are granted access and have created
your account you can visit at any time by following these steps:
1. Log into vena.io.
2. Select Help from the top-right corner. This launches Vena Help.
3. Select Vena Academy from the top of the chatbot window.
4. Select SIGN UP OR LOG IN in the upper-right corner.
