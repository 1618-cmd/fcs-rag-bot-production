Product UpdatesSelecting a local language from a drop-down menu provides flexibility and inclusivity, ensuring
users can engage with the software in the language they’re most comfortable with.
Before you begin
To follow the instructions in this guide, you will need an active Vena account and permission to
access the Contributor/Task tab.
How to
Set a local language

1. Sign in to vena.io and navigate to the Contributor/Tasks tab.
2. Use the drop-down menu to select your preferred language.
