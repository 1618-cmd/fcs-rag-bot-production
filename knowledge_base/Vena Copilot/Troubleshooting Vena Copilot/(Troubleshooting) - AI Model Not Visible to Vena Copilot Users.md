Button Is Not VisibleReach out to your AI Model Manager to do the following:
1. Navigate to the Modeler tab.
2. Select Data Transformations from the sidebar.
3. Select AI Builder from the sidebar tab.
4. Find the AI model in your list of AI models.
5. Ensure that the AI Model is published. If it is unpublished, either right-click on the row or
select the vertical ellipsis for the AI model and select Publish.
6. Ensure the user(s) have been granted the necessary access to the AI model.
7. Once completed, ask your user to refresh their screen or log out and log back into Vena.
