Vena CopilotTroubleshooting: Vena Copilot Prompt
Is Outside of This Model’s Scope
Issue summary
When interacting with Vena Copilot, you may receive the following error message: Your prompt is
outside of this model's Scope. Please update the Scope, submit a different prompt or reach out to
your AI Model Manager.

Error Code 1012 – Data Model Is
Out of SyncSuggested solution
You can do one of two things:
1. Submit another prompt that falls within the Scope.
2. Reach out to your AI Model Manager to update the Scope. Visit the How-To article on
Managing Scopes in Vena Copilot for more information.
Cause
This may occur if you ask questions that fall outside the Scope of the AI model.
