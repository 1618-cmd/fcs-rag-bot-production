How do I launch each chatbot?
Vena Copilot
To launch Vena Copilot you need:
A Vena Copilot license for your tenant.
To be assigned the Vena Copilot user role by an Admin user.
To be assigned permissions to an AI Model by an AI Model Manager.
If you meet all the criteria above, navigate to the Vena Copilot tab and start chatting from the
Chat page in the sidebar.

How do I switch between chatbots?
You cannot use Vena Copilot in panel view and Vena Help at the same time. If Vena Copilot is
open in panel view and you launch Vena Help, you'll be prompted to confirm the switch.
To switch from Vena Help to Vena Copilot, minimize the Vena Help chatbot and go to the Vena
Copilot tab. To keep your Vena Help conversation open, use Vena Copilot in full-page view.
Switching to panel view will prompt you to switch from the Vena Help chatbot to Vena Copilot.
