Suggested solution
1. Navigate to the Modeler tab.
2. Locate the dimension member you are trying to preview intersections for.
3. Remove any special characters from the member name.
4. Try previewing intersections again to confirm that the issue is resolved.
Cause
This can be caused by special characters in the dimension member's name.
