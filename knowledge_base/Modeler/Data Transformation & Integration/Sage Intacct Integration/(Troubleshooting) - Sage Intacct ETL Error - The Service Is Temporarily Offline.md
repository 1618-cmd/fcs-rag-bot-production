Suggested solution
1. Check Intacct's status page at https://status.sage.com/ for any possible incidents. Look for
the region corresponding to your Vena Hub (e.g., US, Canada, Europe).
2. If there's an incident, try re-running the ETL job after the incident has been resolved by Sage.
3. If there are no known incidents with Sage and the issue persists, please reach out to Vena
Support.
