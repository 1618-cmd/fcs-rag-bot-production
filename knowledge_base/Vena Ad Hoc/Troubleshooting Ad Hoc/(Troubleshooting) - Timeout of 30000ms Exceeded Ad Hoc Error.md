Suggested solution
Change the query to a smaller slice of the cube so that the overall scope of the data is reduced.
Cause
Vena Ad Hoc is designed to time out after 30 seconds. If you request more data than can be
processed within that period, Vena Ad Hoc will inform you that it has timed out.
